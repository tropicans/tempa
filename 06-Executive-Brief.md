# TEMPA Executive Brief

## Vision

TEMPA is an AI-augmented capability development platform that transforms civil service learning from classroom-centered training into a structured organizational problem-solving system.

## Strategic Thesis

The platform creates a continuous loop of:

`Problem -> Decision -> Action -> Reflection -> Evidence`

This allows institutions to connect learning investment with real organizational challenges, measurable implementation outcomes, and stronger talent visibility.

## What Makes TEMPA Different

1. AI is used for decision augmentation, not autonomous decision making.
2. Learning is anchored in real institutional problems.
3. Competency growth is measured through project evidence, not only course completion.
4. The platform can evolve from institutional pilot to enterprise and national talent intelligence.

## Core Value By Stakeholder

### For Participants
- experiential learning,
- stronger decision quality,
- guided reflection and growth evidence.

### For Institutions
- better linkage between training and organizational outcomes,
- reusable evidence of project impact,
- improved visibility into capability development.

### For Government Leadership
- measurable capability development,
- stronger innovation pipeline,
- future-ready talent intelligence foundation.

## Recommended Executive Decision

Approve TEMPA as a phased platform initiative with a tightly scoped MVP first. The first release should validate learning workflow, mentor governance, AI usefulness, and TAI credibility before committing to national-scale architecture and analytics expansion.

## Immediate Sponsor Priorities

1. Approve MVP scope.
2. Approve governance model for AI and scoring.
3. Approve pilot institution and target cohort.
4. Approve delivery team and phased roadmap.
